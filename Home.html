Aware of the crucial issues related to climate change, we have recently undertaken significant efforts to minimize our ecological footprint. The owner has enhanced the energy efficiency of the establishment by renovating the insulation in all rooms, thereby promoting more responsible consumption.

In line with our commitment to exceptional hospitality, we highlight an offering distinguished by a warm welcome and simple yet clean and comfortable rooms. We believe in the importance of creating an environment where our guests feel at home while appreciating the ecological improvements we have made to our facilities. Our dedicated team is here to provide you with a memorable experience, combining the best of comfort and sustainability. We look forward to welcoming you to our hotel, where the well-being of our guests and the planet is at the heart of our mission.
